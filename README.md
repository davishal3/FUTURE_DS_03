# 📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

A complete data analytics project focused on understanding marketing funnel behavior and improving conversion rates using real-world campaign data.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes how users move through a marketing funnel — from initial contact to final conversion — and identifies key drop-off points, high-performing segments, and actionable strategies to improve business outcomes.

The analysis simulates real-world work done by marketing analysts, growth teams, and data professionals.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze funnel stages: Total → Contacted → Engaged → Converted
* Calculate conversion rates at each stage
* Identify drop-off points in the funnel
* Compare performance across:

  * Customer segments
  * Campaigns
  * Contact methods
  * Time (monthly trends)
* Provide data-driven business recommendations

## 📊 Funnel Definition

The funnel is constructed as:

1. Total Users
2. Contacted Users
3. Engaged Users (based on interaction duration)
4. Converted Users (subscription = yes)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Significant drop-off occurs before the engagement stage
* Conversion rates vary across job segments
* Contact method has a strong impact on conversions
* Increasing campaign calls does not guarantee success
* Seasonal trends affect performance

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Improve customer engagement quality
* Target high-converting customer segments
* Optimize campaigns based on time trends
* Use effective communication channels
* Reduce excessive repeated campaign contacts
